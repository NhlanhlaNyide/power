Powerpoint Lifestyles (Pty) Ltd is a 100% black owned company based in Durban, KwaZulu Natal. The company was formally established in 2015 as a platform to promote career development within schools and higher education and training sector. It later expanded its scope of practice by providing HR solutions and Industrial Psychology services to both the private and public sector. The expansion in scope of practice has strengthened our service delivery model. 

All our employees hold recognised and credible qualifications in either the field of Human Resources and/or Industrial Psychology

Our staff members are registered with various associations and boards, thus ensuring ethical and professional services

OUR CREDENTIALS

We provide flexible and comprehensive services suitable for each client at a cost-effective rate

The founders have diverse experience in managing a business and ensuring credible business practices.
